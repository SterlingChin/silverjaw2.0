Work

I currently work on the Client Apps team at Podium. Our team oversees our monolith frontend application. We focus on performance and reliability through creating patterns and processes that our engineering teams use in their day to day.

I helped increase the performance of Podium by 90%, bringing down the LCP(Largest Contentful Paint) from 9s to a .9s. Part of the solution lay in our GraphQL calls and utilizing Apollo Cache. I reduced the number of necessary calls from 112 to 32. More can be read here: Reducing GraphQL calls to increase performance

I am also on the leadership team for our Frontend Guild where I lead trainings, discussions, and answer questions around Javascript and React. I try and foster a welcoming environment for all level engineers.
